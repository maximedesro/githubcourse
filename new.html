Quebec Gaming Convention 2026

Join us for the most exciting gaming convention of the year! The Quebec Gaming Convention returns to the iconic Videotron Center on March 7, 2026. Experience three days of non-stop gaming action, industry talks, hands-on workshops, and meet fellow gaming enthusiasts from around the world.

Event Details

📅 Date & Location

Date: March 07, 2026
Venue: Videotron Center, Quebec City
Pricing: $100 per day per attendee

Featured Games

Retro Gaming Tournament

Compete in classic NES and SNES games! Test your skills in Super Mario Bros., Street Fighter II, and The Legend of Zelda speedrun challenges. Prizes for top finishers!

Modern Gaming Showcase

Get hands-on with the latest AAA titles and indie darlings. Exclusive demos of upcoming releases, VR experiences, and meet-and-greets with game developers.

E-Sports Arena

Watch professional teams compete in League of Legends, Counter-Strike, and Rocket League. Live commentary, player interviews, and championship finals throughout the weekend.
